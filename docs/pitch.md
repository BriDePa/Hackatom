# IRRAFRESH / PHASE Pitch

PHASE (Post-Harvest Analysis & Simulation Engine) is the scientific simulation engine inside IRRAFRESH: a decision-support platform for post-harvest logistics of irradiated agricultural products.

For farmers, exporters, and technical evaluators, the platform shows how gamma irradiation, cold-chain quality, transport delays, temperature, humidity, and route stress can shift relative shelf-life, quality, logistics risk, and economic loss.

The platform is a hackathon MVP. It uses simplified models inspired by scientific literature and institutional guidance from IAEA, FAO, WHO, USDA/FDA, and post-harvest shelf-life studies.

## Demo Story

1. Select tomato or strawberry.
2. Choose an abstract logistics profile.
3. Adjust gamma dose, temperature, humidity, transport time, and lot value.
4. Trigger a cold-chain failure event for a defined number of hours.
5. Compare irradiated vs non-irradiated deterioration curves.
6. Read relative risk, uncertainty, economic loss, potential savings, and recommendations.

## What It Is

- Scientific simulation MVP
- Agricultural DSS
- Simplified digital twin of a transported lot
- Agritech and nuclear-tech dashboard
- Comparative scenario engine

## What It Is Not

- Certified food safety tool
- Exact shelf-life predictor
- Industrial AI model
- Nuclear physics simulator
- Replacement for laboratory validation
